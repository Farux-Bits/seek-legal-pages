Child Safety Standards

SEEK Child Safety Standards (CSAE/CSAM)

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Our Commitment

SEEK has zero tolerance for any content, behavior, or activity involving child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE/CSAM).

This includes sexual content involving minors, grooming, sexual solicitation, trafficking, or any attempt to share, request, or distribute CSAM.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on SEEK:

  • any sexual content involving minors, including images, videos, text, audio, or links
  • grooming, sexual solicitation, or sexualized conversation with a minor
  • content that sexualizes minors, even if not explicit
  • requests for sexual images or sexual contact from a minor
  • sharing links to external CSAM material or communities
  • any content that promotes, normalizes, or facilitates child exploitation

3. Reporting Child Safety Concerns

Users can report content or accounts inside the app using the Report and Block tools.

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services immediately.

You can also report concerns directly to us at:

Email: faruxbits@gmail.com

Subject line suggestion: CSAE/Child Safety Report

Please include: username(s), links or screenshots, and any relevant details.

4. Enforcement Actions

If we detect or receive reports of CSAE/CSAM-related content or behavior, SEEK may:

  • remove the content immediately
  • suspend or permanently ban the account or accounts involved
  • restrict device access and prevent re-registration where feasible
  • preserve relevant information where required for legal compliance

5. CSAM Handling and Legal Cooperation

SEEK takes CSAE/CSAM reports seriously and cooperates with law enforcement when legally required.

Where applicable, we may report suspected CSAE/CSAM to appropriate authorities and organizations that handle such reports.

6. Moderation and Prevention

SEEK uses a combination of methods to prevent and respond to CSAE/CSAM risks, including:

  • user reporting and rapid review workflows
  • account and content enforcement for policy violations
  • measures to detect repeat offenders and abusive behavior patterns

7. Age Requirements

SEEK is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that an account belongs to a user under the minimum age, we will take action, which may include account removal.
