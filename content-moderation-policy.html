Content Moderation Policy

SEEK Content Moderation Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

SEEK is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful community. This Content Moderation Policy explains how SEEK reviews and moderates user-generated content on the platform.

Content moderation is used to enforce SEEK's Community Guidelines and protect users from harmful or abusive content.

2. Content Subject to Moderation

Content that may be moderated includes:

  • photo posts and pins
  • captions and text attached to posts
  • comments and replies
  • hunt challenges and submissions
  • hidden gem posts
  • user reports and appeals

Moderation may occur before or after content is visible on the platform.

3. Moderation Methods

SEEK uses a combination of moderation systems.

Automated Moderation

Automated systems analyze content to detect potential violations.

These systems may review:

  • text content
  • images
  • metadata associated with posts

Automated moderation may temporarily limit visibility or flag content for review.

User Reporting

Users can report content that violates SEEK rules.

Reported content is reviewed by moderation systems and may be escalated to manual review.

Manual Review

Moderators may review content flagged by automated systems or reported by users.

Moderators evaluate:

  • the context of the content
  • the severity of the violation
  • past violations associated with the user

4. Enforcement Actions

When a violation is confirmed, SEEK may take one or more of the following actions:

  • remove the content
  • issue a warning to the user
  • temporarily restrict posting privileges
  • suspend certain features
  • permanently suspend the account

Severe violations may result in immediate account termination.

5. Repeat Violations

Users who repeatedly violate SEEK policies may face escalating penalties.

These may include:

  • temporary restrictions
  • extended suspensions
  • permanent bans

Repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.

6. Appeals Process

Users may request a review of moderation decisions.

Appeals allow users to challenge:

  • removed content
  • account restrictions
  • suspensions

Appeals are reviewed by the moderation team when possible.

7. Transparency

SEEK strives to apply moderation rules consistently and fairly.

Moderation systems may evolve over time to improve safety and community standards.
