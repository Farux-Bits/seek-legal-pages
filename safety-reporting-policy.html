Safety & Reporting Policy

SEEK Safety & Reporting Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At SEEK, the safety of our community is extremely important. This Safety & Reporting Policy explains how users can report harmful content or behavior and how SEEK responds to safety concerns.

Our goal is to create a platform where people can safely share experiences, discover locations, and interact with others.

1. Reporting Harmful Content

If you encounter content that violates SEEK's rules or makes you feel unsafe, you can report it within the app.

Content that can be reported includes:

  • posts
  • comments
  • user profiles
  • direct messages
  • hunts or location-based challenges
  • hidden gem submissions

Reports allow our moderation systems to review and take action where necessary.

2. Types of Violations

Users should report content that includes:

Harassment or Abuse

  • threats or intimidation
  • bullying
  • repeated unwanted contact

Hate Speech

  • content attacking people based on race, religion, gender, nationality, disability, or sexual orientation

Illegal Activity

  • promotion of illegal services
  • criminal activity
  • stolen goods

Child Safety Violations

  • exploitation or abuse involving minors
  • attempts to contact minors for harmful purposes
  • any sexual content involving minors

Dangerous Content

  • promotion of self-harm
  • encouragement of violence
  • dangerous challenges

Spam or Fraud

  • scams
  • misleading promotions
  • fake accounts or impersonation

3. How to Report Content

Users can report content directly through the SEEK app.

Typical reporting steps include:

  • Select the Report option on the content or profile.
  • Choose the reason for the report.
  • Optionally include additional details.

Reports are submitted to the SEEK moderation system for review.

4. Moderation Process

SEEK uses multiple systems to review reported content.

Moderation methods may include:

  • automated moderation tools
  • machine-learning based content analysis
  • manual moderator review
  • investigation of user reports

These systems help detect policy violations and keep the community safe.

5. Enforcement Actions

When content violates SEEK policies, we may take actions including:

  • removing the content
  • issuing warnings to the user
  • temporarily restricting features
  • suspending accounts
  • permanently banning accounts

The severity of the action depends on the nature of the violation.

Repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.

6. Blocking and Safety Controls

SEEK provides safety tools that allow users to control their experience.

Users may:

  • block other users
  • mute conversations
  • hide content
  • report abusive behavior

These tools help users manage unwanted interactions.

7. Child Safety Commitment

SEEK has zero tolerance for child exploitation or abuse.

Content involving the sexual exploitation of minors is strictly prohibited.

Accounts found engaging in such activity will be permanently banned and may be reported to relevant authorities.

8. Law Enforcement Requests

In certain cases, SEEK may cooperate with lawful requests from authorities.

This may occur when:

  • required by law
  • necessary to investigate serious safety threats
  • related to illegal activity on the platform

Such requests are handled in accordance with applicable laws.

9. False Reports

Submitting false or abusive reports may interfere with the safety system.

Users who repeatedly submit false reports may face restrictions.

10. Emergency Situations

SEEK is not an emergency service.

If you are in immediate danger or require emergency assistance, please contact local emergency services.

11. Appeals

Users may contact support if they believe moderation actions were taken incorrectly.

Appeals may be reviewed by the moderation team.

12. Continuous Safety Improvements

SEEK continuously works to improve its safety systems.

This may include:

  • improving automated moderation tools
  • updating community guidelines
  • expanding reporting features
