SEEK Copyright Policy (DMCA)

Last updated: March 2026

1. Respect for Copyright

SEEK respects intellectual property rights and expects users to do the same.

Users may only upload content they own or have permission to use.

2. Reporting Copyright Violations

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used without permission on SEEK, you may submit a copyright complaint.

Your report should include:

  • your contact information
  • identification of the copyrighted work
  • identification of the infringing content
  • a statement that you believe the use is unauthorized
  • a statement that the information provided is accurate

3. Content Removal

When a valid copyright complaint is received, SEEK may:

  • remove the reported content
  • notify the user who uploaded the content
  • restrict the user's account for repeated violations

4. Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly violate copyright rules may have their accounts permanently suspended.

5. Counter Notification

Users who believe their content was removed by mistake may submit a counter-notification.

The moderation team will review the claim when appropriate.
