Acceptable Use Policy

SEEK Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy describes permitted and prohibited uses of SEEK.

Users must follow this policy when using the platform.

2. Allowed Use

Users may use SEEK to:

  • capture and share photos
  • explore locations
  • interact with other users
  • participate in hunts and discovery features

All activity must comply with applicable laws.

3. Prohibited Activities

Users may not use SEEK to:

  • violate any laws or regulations
  • harass or threaten other users
  • distribute illegal content
  • impersonate individuals or organizations
  • spread malicious software
  • conduct scams or fraudulent activities
  • operate automated bots without permission
  • manipulate engagement or platform features

4. Platform Integrity

Users may not attempt to:

  • interfere with the operation of the platform
  • reverse engineer the application
  • bypass security systems
  • access restricted data

5. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • warnings
  • removal of content
  • suspension of features
  • termination of accounts
