Data Retention & Deletion Policy

SEEK Data Retention & Deletion Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This policy explains how SEEK stores, retains, and deletes user data.

SEEK retains data only for as long as necessary to operate the platform and maintain security.

2. Account Data

Account data may include:

  • email address
  • user identifier
  • profile information
  • authentication records

If a user requests account deletion:

  • the account is disabled immediately
  • permanent deletion occurs after approximately 30 days

This delay allows users to reverse accidental deletions.

3. User Content

User-generated content includes:

  • posts
  • photos
  • captions
  • comments
  • messages

Content may remain stored until it is deleted by the user or removed through moderation.

Deleted content may remain temporarily in backups for security purposes.

4. Temporary Verification Data

Certain features may require temporary verification images or media.

These items are retained only for a short period and may be automatically deleted after approximately 7 days.

5. Location Data

Location information may be stored for:

  • posts
  • travel history
  • discovery features

Location data associated with posts remains stored while the content exists.

6. Safety and Moderation Records

To protect the community, SEEK may retain moderation records including:

  • reports submitted by users
  • moderation decisions
  • safety investigations

These records may be stored longer than normal user data to prevent abuse.

7. Device and Notification Data

Device information such as push notification tokens may be stored to deliver notifications.

These records may be deleted when:

  • the user logs out
  • the account is deleted
  • tokens become inactive

8. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of their accounts through the app settings.

After the deletion request:

  • account access is disabled immediately
  • permanent deletion occurs after the retention period

Some data may remain temporarily in backups or legal logs.

9. Updates

This policy may be updated as SEEK evolves.
