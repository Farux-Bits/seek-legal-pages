Community Guidelines

SEEK Community Guidelines

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to SEEK.

SEEK is a platform where people share photos, discover places, and explore the world through location-based posts. Our goal is to create a safe and respectful community where everyone can enjoy sharing and discovering content.

These Community Guidelines explain what is allowed and what is not allowed on SEEK.

All users must follow these rules when using the platform.

1. Respect Other Users

SEEK is a community built on respect.

You must not:

  • Harass, bully, or threaten other users
  • Send abusive or insulting messages
  • Target users based on race, religion, gender, nationality, disability, or sexual orientation
  • Encourage others to attack or harass someone

We want SEEK to remain welcoming to everyone.

2. No Illegal Content

You may not use SEEK to post or promote illegal content.

This includes:

  • criminal activity
  • illegal services or products
  • stolen property
  • instructions for committing crimes

Content that violates local or international law may be removed and reported if necessary.

3. Child Safety

SEEK has zero tolerance for content involving the exploitation of minors.

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • sexual content involving minors
  • grooming or attempts to contact minors for harmful purposes
  • sharing or requesting child sexual abuse material

Accounts involved in such activities will be immediately banned and reported to appropriate authorities.

4. Violence and Dangerous Content

Content that promotes violence or harm is not allowed.

This includes:

  • threats of violence
  • encouragement of physical harm
  • graphic violence
  • dangerous challenges that may cause injury

Content that glorifies violence may also be removed.

5. Hate Speech and Discrimination

SEEK does not allow hate speech.

You may not post content that:

  • promotes hatred toward groups of people
  • insults individuals based on protected characteristics
  • promotes extremist or hateful ideologies

We aim to maintain a respectful environment for all users.

6. Harassment and Bullying

You may not use SEEK to harass or intimidate others.

Examples include:

  • repeated unwanted messages
  • stalking behavior
  • public shaming or humiliation
  • encouraging others to harass someone

Users experiencing harassment should report the content.

7. Privacy and Personal Information

Respect the privacy of others.

Do not post:

  • private phone numbers
  • home addresses
  • private email addresses
  • identification documents
  • sensitive personal information

Sharing private information without consent may result in account suspension.

8. Impersonation

You may not impersonate:

  • another user
  • a public figure
  • a company or organization

Profiles that mislead others about identity may be removed.

9. Spam and Manipulation

SEEK is not a platform for spam.

You may not:

  • repeatedly post promotional content
  • send mass unsolicited messages
  • manipulate engagement using bots or fake accounts

Accounts engaging in spam may be restricted or removed.

10. Misleading or False Content

You may not post content intended to deceive users.

Examples include:

  • fake locations
  • misleading descriptions
  • fraudulent activities

Content intended to trick or exploit users may be removed.

11. Location Safety

SEEK allows users to attach locations to posts.

When sharing location-based content:

  • avoid posting precise private locations (such as homes)
  • respect restricted or sensitive areas
  • follow local laws when capturing photos

Users should be mindful of safety when sharing locations.

12. Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines, you can report it within the app.

Reports help us keep the platform safe.

Our moderation team reviews reports and may take actions such as:

  • removing content
  • issuing warnings
  • restricting accounts
  • suspending accounts
  • permanently banning users

13. Moderation Systems

SEEK uses a combination of:

  • automated moderation systems
  • user reports
  • manual review by moderators

These systems help detect harmful or abusive content.

However, no system is perfect, and user reports remain important.

14. Enforcement Actions

If a user violates these guidelines, we may take actions including:

  • content removal
  • warnings
  • temporary restrictions
  • suspension of features
  • permanent account removal

Repeated violations may lead to permanent bans.

15. Appeals

If you believe a moderation decision was made incorrectly, you may contact support to request a review.

Appeals are evaluated by our moderation team.

16. Updates to These Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines as SEEK evolves.

When changes are made, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.
